OVERVIEW

This program establish a fundamental understanding of politics, government and public administration. It prescribes the basic concepts and techniques required to examine political structures, policies, and such social issues as at the national and international levels. The program includes critical thinking, research experience and application of the knowledge in the sphere of political science.

EXPECTED ACTIVITIES

Policy analysis projects: Analyzing environmental policy proposals, like those addressing climate change, and evaluating their potential impacts on the economy and society.
Debates and discussions: Debating the merits and drawbacks of a recent foreign policy decision, or discussing the implications of new election laws.
Field trips and internships: Visiting the local government offices to learn about legislative processes, or interning with a political campaign to understand election strategies.
