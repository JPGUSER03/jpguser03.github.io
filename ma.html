OVERVIEW

This program emphasizes theoretical principles in mathematics, as well as practical applications of mathematics, scientific computation, and computer science fundamentals. It teaches students how to intelligently solve problems and handle mathematical computations in a computing context. This degree provides students to work in data analysis, software development, or pursue graduate study in either sector.

EXPECTED ACTIVITIES

Problem-solving exercises: Solving complex calculus problems to understand rate changes or working on combinatorics puzzles to improve logical thinking.
Mathematical modeling projects: Creating a model to predict population growth in a given area, or using statistics to analyze data for a local business to optimize inventory.
Research papers and presentations: Writing a paper on applications of cryptography in data security and presenting the findings to classmates.
