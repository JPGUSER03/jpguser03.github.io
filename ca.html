OVERVIEW

This degree incorporates media and communication theories as well as hands on training in writing, broadcasting and media. production. The holders of the degree can find employment in the such industries as journalism, media, public relations, or new media.

EXPECTED ACTIVITIES

Public speaking and presentation exercises: Delivering a persuasive speech on a current social issue, like climate action or mental health awareness, to practice audience engagement.
Media production projects: Producing a short documentary on a community event, creating a social media campaign for a nonprofit, or designing a magazine layout.
Internships with media outlets: Working at a newspaper to learn journalistic writing, interning at a radio station, or assisting with social media content at a PR firm.
