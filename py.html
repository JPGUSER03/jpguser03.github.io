OVERVIEW

This degree program takes four years and deals with the behavior, mental processes, and feelings of individuals. Through the offers a wide range of approaches in psychological theories and in available research, preparing graduates for professions in human resources, education, or for the purpose of transitioning to masters programmes in medicine, counselling or clinical psychology.

EXPECTED ACTIVITIES

Laboratory experiments: Conducting experiments on memory recall to understand cognitive processes, or observing behavioral responses in a controlled setting.
Counseling role-playing exercises: Simulating counseling sessions with classmates to practice active listening, empathy, and therapeutic responses.
Fieldwork and internships: Assisting a school counselor with student support sessions, or interning in a corporate setting to observe workplace psychology applications.
