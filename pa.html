OVERVIEW

The purpose of this program is to develop knowledge on the part of the client in the field of public policy, governance and administration. Starting from the government processes, students discover how policies are developed, and the problems facing the public sector to train them as government, nongovernmental, or community workers.

EXPECTED ACTIVITIES

Case studies of public policies: Reviewing and analyzing a city’s public transportation policy to assess its effectiveness and propose improvements.
Community service projects: Organizing a community clean-up event or assisting with local food distribution to experience public service firsthand.
Internships in government or nonprofit organizations: Working in a mayor’s office to understand municipal operations or interning at a nonprofit to support program development and management.
